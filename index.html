Avacado Egg Toast

Our avacado egg toast is the the most delicate crispy piece of bread toasted to perfection paired with the freshest avacados the west coast has to offer and home grown eggs from right here in the valley.

Spaghetti

Our spaghetti is rolled for 3 hours before cooking so our dough can fully rise before it even begins to look like spaghetti. From there our home chefs make magic and take our home fresh noodles to perfection. And then we put Ragu on it.

Dessert

Enjoy a decadent slice of cake crafted by the wholesome hands of our home town bakers. Our chocoate cake features a buttercream chocolate frosting with a crisp cool texture as well as an angel food chocolate center.

Cocktail Drinks

We offer the fanciest drinks you can possibly imagine. Have you ever had a drink with exotic leaves and flowers in it? Have you ever had a sip of liquid gold? You can have those things here at Our Restaurant Has Tables.
